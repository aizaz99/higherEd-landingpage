About the platform

Designed for students, families, and future applicants

Campus Connect is a realistic higher education website project that demonstrates professional information architecture, responsive design, accessibility, and client-side interactivity.

Mission

The goal of this platform is to make campus information easier to find, easier to understand, and easier to use across devices.

Audience

The main audience includes prospective students, transfer students, current students, and families looking for clear academic and support information.

Design Principles

The layout uses contrast, repetition, alignment, and proximity to create a consistent, readable, and professional experience.

Project Highlights

  • Accessible semantic structure
  • Responsive multi-page navigation
  • Higher ed content organization
  • Reusable cards, buttons, forms, and layout patterns

Information Architecture

Hierarchical navigation with Home, About, Programs, Resources, and Contact pages.

Responsive Strategy

Mobile-first layout that scales smoothly across three breakpoints.

Accessibility

Keyboard support, skip links, visible focus states, semantic landmarks, and readable contrast.
